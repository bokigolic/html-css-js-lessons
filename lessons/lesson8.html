📨 What Are Forms?

Forms collect user input—login info, comments, surveys, and more. They're essential for making your site interactive.

💡 Accessibility Tip: Always use `

🧾 Basic Form Structure

<form action="/submit" method="post">
  <label for="name">Name:</label>
  <input type="text" id="name" name="name" required />
  <button type="submit">Submit</button>
</form>

Note: `action` tells the browser where to send data, `method="post"` keeps it secure.

🔢 Common Input Types

  • text – Free text
  • password – Masked input
  • email – Validated email format
  • checkbox – Multiple selections
  • radio – Single choice group
  • submit – Submit button
<input type="email" id="email" name="email" required />
<input type="checkbox" id="subscribe" name="subscribe" />
<label for="subscribe">Subscribe to newsletter</label>
💡 UX Tip: Use `required`, `placeholder`, and validation patterns to guide users.

📝 Labels and Textareas

<label for="bio">Your bio:</label>
<textarea id="bio" name="bio" rows="4" cols="30" required></textarea>
