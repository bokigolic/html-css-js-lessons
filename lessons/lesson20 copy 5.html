📨 What Are Forms?

Forms collect user input—login info, comments, surveys, and more. They're essential for making your site interactive.

🧾 Basic Form Structure

<form action="/submit" method="post">
  <label for="name">Name:</label>
  <input type="text" id="name" name="name" required />
  <button type="submit">Submit</button>
</form>

🔢 Common Input Types

  • text – Free text
  • email – Validated email format
  • password – Masked input

🧪 Example Form

<form>
  <input type="email" name="email" required />
  <button>Send</button>
</form>

📌 Summary

  • Use <form>, <input>, <label>, and friends
