🖼️ Why Images and Multimedia Matter

Media brings websites to life. 📷 Well-used images, 🎥 videos, and 🎧 audio deliver richer experiences, boost engagement, and help tell stories more effectively.

💡 Pro Tip: Always optimize media assets for size and performance—nobody likes slow-loading pages!

📷 Adding Images with <img>

Use the <img> tag to embed images:

<img src="cat.jpg" alt="Cute smiling cat" />
  • src – image location (relative or absolute URL)
  • alt – descriptive text for screen readers and when images fail to load
Accessibility: Good alt text is crucial for users with visual impairment.

📱 Making Images Responsive

Ensure your images scale well on any device:

<img src="landscape.jpg" alt="Mountain landscape" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />
SEO Tip: Smaller, responsive images improve load speed and search engine rankings.

🖼️ Mini Image Gallery Example

<h2>My Travel Gallery</h2>
<div class="gallery">
  <img src="rome.jpg" alt="Colosseum, Rome" width="300" />
  <img src="paris.jpg" alt="Eiffel Tower at night" width="300" />
  <img src="nyc.jpg" alt="New York skyline" width="300" />
</div>
Performance Tip: Use modern formats like WebP and lazy loading for faster load times.

🎥 Embedding Video with <video>

Use the <video> tag with controls and fallback content:

<video width="320" height="240" controls>
  <source src="intro.mp4" type="video/mp4" />
  Your browser does not support the video tag.
</video>
  • controls – show player interface
  • autoplay, loop, muted – additional behaviors
📌 Note: Add captions or transcripts for accessibility and SEO benefits.

🎧 Embedding Audio with <audio>

Play audio using the <audio> tag:

<audio controls>
  <source src="soundtrack.mp3" type="audio/mpeg" />
  Your browser does not support the audio element.
</audio>
Accessibility: Provide captioning or transcripts for audio content.

🧪 Practice: Media Showcase

Create a media showcase using all three formats:

<h2>🎮 My Media Showcase</h2>
<img src="console.jpg" alt="Gaming console" />
<video src="gameplay.mp4" controls muted loop></video>
<audio src="game-music.mp3" controls></audio>

📌 Summary

  • <img>: images with alt are essential for accessibility
  • Use style or responsive attributes for flexible design
  • <video> and <audio>: add player controls and captions
  • Optimize media for performance: formats, compression, lazy-load
